Our Gas Products

We supply all kinds of Industrial gases.

Liquid Nitrogen

Liquid nitrogen is a clear, scentless, and very cold liquid state of nitrogen gas (N₂). It is a cryogenic liquid, indicating it is kept at an extremely low temperature to stay in its liquid form. The boiling point of liquid nitrogen is around −196°C (−321°F) under atmospheric pressure.

Carbon Dioxide Gas

Carbon dioxide (CO2) is a liquefiable, colorless, non-flammable gas that can be obtained from a variety of different sources. Carbon dioxide has many well-known applications in the food and beverages industry – from carbonating drinks and conserving wine to modifying atmospheres for packaged foods.

Nitrogen Gas

Nitrogen (N2) is a colorless, odorless, tasteless, non-toxic and almost totally inert gas. It is produced in high volumes at air separation plants. Nitrogen is likewise vital to the chemical sector. It is utilized in the manufacturing of fertilizers, nitric acid, nylon, dyes, and explosives.
